How It Works

For Senders (You)

  1. Track your email with track_email.py
  2. Generate the button with generate_tracking_button.py
  3. Copy and paste the HTML code into your email
  4. Send the email

For Recipients

  1. Receive email with tracking button
  2. Click "View Evidence on Public Ledge" button
  3. See the verified record on the public ledger
  4. Your click is tracked (via web analytics)
